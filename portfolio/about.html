About Me

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Tony Edson, a Senior Software Developer with over 10 years of experience creating scalable and maintainable software solutions. I specialize in backend development, system architecture, and leading teams to deliver impactful projects. My goal is to transform ideas into robust applications while fostering collaboration and innovation.

Professional Expertise

  • Backend Development: Expertise in programming languages such as PHP, Kotlin, and TypeScript.
  • Software Architecture: Proficient in designing modular systems using patterns like Ports and Adapters.
  • Agile Leadership: Experienced in Scrum and Kanban, mentoring teams to meet deadlines with high-quality results.
  • Database Management: Skilled in optimizing relational and non-relational databases like MySQL, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL.
  • DevOps Tools: Proficient in Docker, Git, and CI/CD pipelines for efficient development workflows.

Personal Values and Work Philosophy

I believe that technology should empower people and businesses. My philosophy revolves around writing clean, maintainable code and continuously learning new skills to stay ahead in the fast-evolving tech landscape. Collaboration and innovation are at the heart of my work, as I strive to deliver solutions that exceed expectations.

Hobbies and Interests

Outside of coding, I enjoy spending time with my family, reading about personal development, and exploring new technologies. I’m also passionate about mentoring junior developers and sharing knowledge through team workshops.
